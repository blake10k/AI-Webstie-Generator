Deploy to Vercel

Deploy your website to a live URL for free using your GitHub repository.

  1. Log in with GitHub.
  2. Click "Add New Project".
  3. Select your new repository.
  4. Click "Deploy". Your site will be live!

Add a Free Domain

Get a free custom domain and link it to your newly deployed Vercel project.

  1. Find a domain on Freenom.
  2. Register and manage DNS.
  3. In Vercel, go to Project → Settings → Domains.
  4. Add your domain and follow the instructions.
